Calzone

Calendar timezone proxy that just works.

The Problem

Outlook exports calendars with Windows timezone names. Google Calendar expects IANA standard names. Events appear at wrong times—typically off by 1 hour.

The Solution

Replace outlook.office365.com with calzone.mooracle.io in your calendar URL.

# Before https://outlook.office365.com/owa/calendar/.../calendar.ics # After https://calzone.mooracle.io/owa/calendar/.../calendar.ics

Add to Google Calendar

  1. Open Google Calendar
  2. Click + next to "Other calendars"
  3. Select "From URL"
  4. Paste your calzone URL
  5. Click "Add calendar"
No data stored. No accounts required.
